03 : About me

Who am I ?

My name is Costin Dumitrescu and I am a Full Stack Software Developer from Montreal, Canada. I'm very passionate about programming languages, developing websites, data science, music and traveling. From my point of view, building a website is an art, therefore I always want to go above and beyond when building one. My professional goal is to enhance my expertise and bring value to my prospective employer as well as developing my skills.

Le Wagon bootcamp

Ranked # 1 coding bootcamp worldwide on Switchup, Le Wagon prepared me with enough knowledge to land my first job in the Software Development world. During my study at Le Wagon, I intensively used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, SQL, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Git, GitHub, Heroku, Figma, Agile, React. For the final project,I was part of a team of four people with whom I have built an amazing application that will solve the pain of musicians and venue owners. Working on this project was pleasure because one of my biggest hobbies/passions is writing and performing music. During the two weeks that we had to build this application from scratch, I have collaborated with my three team mates, helped them and worked very well as a team.

Professional Experience

As a Cybersecurity student, I had the chance to be offered a job during my studies as a Full Stack Software developer in a Cybersecurity company. I had the chance to work with technologies such as Python programming language, Flask framework, React.js and MongoDB. For my second experience in the domain, I had the chance to work for a Startup that was building an independent movies streaming platform. I was one of the first developers that created the first version of the application. I also had the chance to show my expertise in Ruby on Rails, which led me to the position of team lead just after a couple of months. I am currently looking for a job in the Technology industry, where I can show my value or learn something new that will challenge me to be the best.
